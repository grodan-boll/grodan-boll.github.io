BIOGRAPHY

KniccKnacc, also known as, Notorious KniccKnacc, is an up-and-coming singer/songwriter/rapper from Stockholm, Sweden. He usually collab on songs with his close childhood friend Vnek. KniccKnacc debut on Spotify with the hit single El Banjo, where he worked with Diedz and Vnek. Diedz is also a very close friend to KniccKnacc and Vnek. KniccKnacc, Vnek, and starplayerr released an EP in 2019 called Black Ice. Starplayerr is again, a close childhood friend of both KniccKnacc and Vnek. He produced the tracks. Later on in 2020 the ‘’trio’’, formed of KniccKnacc, Vnek and starplayerr, decided to release an album in swedish instead of english to better suit their audience. This EP was called 182records and was received plenty better than their prior album Black Ice. In 2021 the group released a single called ‘’Parisienne’’. This was sort of a teaser that there might be coming some more music later on in 2021.
